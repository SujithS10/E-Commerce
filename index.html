A lovely perfume has a charming blend of floral, fruity, and soft musk notes. It opens with fresh citrus or berry accents, followed by delicate florals like rose or jasmine, and finishes with a warm base of vanilla or sandalwood for a captivating touch.

An ocean perfume captures the essence of the sea with fresh, aquatic notes. It opens with crisp citrus and marine accords, evoking a cool breeze. Heart notes of sea salt and light florals add a touch of airiness, while a base of driftwood or musk brings a soothing, coastal depth.

A gangster perfume exudes boldness and sophistication, featuring smoky, spicy, and leathery notes. It opens with a strong hit of black pepper or tobacco, followed by a heart of rich leather or dark woods. The base lingers with deep amber, patchouli, or smoky incense, creating a powerful and daring aura.
